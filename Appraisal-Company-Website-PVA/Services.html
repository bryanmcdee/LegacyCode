Homeowners
Homeowners need appraisals for a variety of reasons including refinance, pre-listing services, asset valuations, and PMI removal.
The most common appraisal ordered directly from a homeowner is a pre-listing appraisal. This is an appraisal to determine the market value of the home for selling purposes. It is hard to be unbiased when you are trying to value your own home because you are emotionally attached to it. An appraiser will give you an unbiased opinion of value to help you set a realistic selling price.
Homeowners are sometimes surprised to find out the market value of their home is more than they thought. An accurate real estate appraisal can help you receive several thousand dollars more than you would have by setting the asking price too low. On the other hand, setting an asking price too high will not attract buyers which means your house will not sell. This wastes time, money, and efforts. An appraisal can help set a realistic price to your house will sell quickly. Getting a professional appraisal before listing a home will help prevent any surprises.
