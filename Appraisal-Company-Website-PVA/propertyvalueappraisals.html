My name is Bryan McDonald and I'm the owner of Property Value Appraisal, Inc.

I graduated from Warner Robins High School right here in Middle GA and graduated from GA Tech with a bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering.

I am a certified real estate appraiser and have over 10 years of real estate appraisal experience. I provide residential real estate appraisal services in Houston and Peach County in Georgia.

I specialize in appraisals for home purchases, refinancing, and construction loans. In addition, I also provide appraisal services for real estate agents or homeowners who need an appraisal to help determine a market value of a house, appraisals for estate planning, bankruptcy, divorce, foreclosures, and PMI removal.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions.
